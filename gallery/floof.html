Floof

About

Breed: American Eskimo

Age: 5 Years Old

Birthday: February 19

House-trained: Yes

Floof was named after his fluffy appearance. He's quick and clever, alert and friendly, and neither shy or aggressive. Floof is the perfect home pet if you're looking for a large cuddle buddy.
Floof does require a little more attention than other dogs as they can develop problem behaviors if undertrained or misteated.
